FAQ

How can I find hotel ?

You can navigate to hotel page and you will find a form determine your destination, check-in date, chek-out date and guest number; then click search button you will get a list of available rooms fit your specifications

How can I book a flight ?

You can navigate to flight page and you will find a form determine your destination, departure date, return date , passenger number and class; then click search button you will get a list of available flights fit your specifications

How can I find a tour ?

You can navigate to tour page and you will find a form determine your destination, start date, duration and number of persons; then click search button you will get a list of available tours fit your specifications

How can I rent a car ?

You can navigate to car page and you will find a form determine your pick-up date, drop-off date, pick-up location and drop-off location; then click search button you will get a list of available cars fit your specifications

How can I find prices reasonable for my budget ?

You can navigate to search and filter page and you will find a filteration form determine your specifications then click apply filter button you will get a list of available options fit your specifications
